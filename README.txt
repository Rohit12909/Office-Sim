Controls:
Move Left: A
Move Right: D
Jump: W

Weapons:
Shoot Staple: I
Throw paper star: O

At elevator/door:
Space to ride

Restart game: esc

Cheats:
Alt + H: full hp
Alt + N: next room
Alt + L: invincibility
Alt + K: give paper throwing stars
